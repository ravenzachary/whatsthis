Settings saved. Here's how to use What's This?

📝
Text

Select any text — a word, sentence, or paragraph. A purple bar appears; wait 1 second and the answer pops up. Or right-click and choose "What's This?"

🖼️
Images

Hover over any image for 1.5 seconds — a pulsing ring appears, then analysis begins. Point at a specific detail to learn about that exact thing.

🎬
Video

Right-click a video and choose "What's This? (Video Frame)" to analyze the current frame.

💬
Follow-ups

Ask up to 4 follow-up questions in each popover to dig deeper into any topic.

No keyboard needed! Just select text or hover over images and videos. Optional shortcuts: F2 or Cmd+Shift+U
